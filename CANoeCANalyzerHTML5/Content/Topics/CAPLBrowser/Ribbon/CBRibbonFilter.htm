GroupFilter

Configuration

  • With a click on the symbol you reset the filter settings to the settings of the CANoe environment.
  • With a click on the symbol you activate all possible filters. All available functions, event handlers and symbols are displayed, regardless of the CANoe environment.
  • With a click on the symbol you deactivate all filters.

 

Activate/deactivate filter

The filters in the CAPL Browser depend on the CANoe/CANalyzer environment and control the visibility of the predefined functions, event handlers and symbols and affect the display in the Function Explorer, the autocomplete input assistance and Symbol Explorer/MOST Symbol Explorer.

If a MOST bus is not used in the CANoe configuration, no MOST functions are displayed.

The protocol and bus system filters (CAN, FlexRay etc.) control the visibility of the predefined functions and event handlers. They affect the Function Explorer and the autocomplete input assistance function.
