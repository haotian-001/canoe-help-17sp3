Isochronous Channel Streaming

 

Figure 1: Block diagram streaming of the isochronous channels

Features of the Streaming via USB

  • 1:1 streaming of isochronous data on the hard disk drive or to an IP Port
  • direct recording of TS files
  • online statistics and metadata of the stream
  • the isochronous data is transmitted unchanged from the MOST ring to the computer
  • efficient implementation on driver level for low CPU pollution

Use Cases

  • recording of video TS stream for offline analysis
  • online analysis of isochronous data
  • online streaming to a media player over the network

There are two universal streaming DLLs provided with your CANoe installation which can be used to record or replay isochronous data. You find additional information in the document MOST_SyncStreaming_Documentation.pdf.
