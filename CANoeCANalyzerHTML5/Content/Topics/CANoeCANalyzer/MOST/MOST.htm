Option MOST

 

MOST (Media Oriented Systems Transport) is a versatile, high-performance multimedia network technology based on synchronous data communication. It is ideal for multimedia applications in the automotive sector (audio, video, navigation and communication).

Application Areas: Automotive engineering (multimedia applications; audio, video, navigation).
