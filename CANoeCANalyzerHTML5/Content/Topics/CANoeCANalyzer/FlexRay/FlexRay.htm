Option FlexRay

 

Option FlexRay offers a wide range of capabilities for the analysis of distributed realtime control systems with FlexRay.

The Vector FlexRay Network Interfaces (PCI, USB, …) are available for the connection to FlexRay networks.

Application areas: Automotive engineering (e.g. security relevant applications, X-By-Wire).
