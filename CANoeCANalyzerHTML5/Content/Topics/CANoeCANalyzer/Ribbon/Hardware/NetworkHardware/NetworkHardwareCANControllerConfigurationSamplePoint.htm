Sample Point

 

The Sample Point is the point of time at which the bus level is read and interpreted as the value of that respective bit. Its location is at the end of TSEG1.

Value

Description

1

The bit is only sampled at the end of TSEG1.

3

The bit is sampled three times.

1st sample point

Two time quanta (Tq) before end of TSEG1.

2nd sample point

One time quantum (Tq) before end of TSEG1.

3rd sample point

At the end of TSEG1.

TSEG1 = PROP_SEG + PHASE_SEG1

TSEG2 = PHASE_SEG2

Note on CAN FD

Sampling will only ever occur once at the end of TSEG1 for CAN FD.
