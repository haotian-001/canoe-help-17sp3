Protocol Information [▲ back]

The Real-time Transport Protocol (RTP) is used to transport media data streams such as video or audio data. For control purposes RTCP protocol is used.

CANoe offers further possibilities to use RTP in Configurations.

Initialization

During initialization of the RTP protocol, the fields SourcePort and DestinationPort in the UDP header are also set to a RTP port number that is configured in the Options dialog.
