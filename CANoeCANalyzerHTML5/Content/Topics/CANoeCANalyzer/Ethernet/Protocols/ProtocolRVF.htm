Raw Video Format (RVF)

 

Quick Access

Header Format

Protocol Information [▲ back]

The Raw Video Format (RVF) is designed to carry direct mappings of raw video data streams. It is a primary objective of RVF to allow all kinds of video rasters to support TSN-based transport of raw video payloads, with a minimum degree of added complexity or transcoding.
