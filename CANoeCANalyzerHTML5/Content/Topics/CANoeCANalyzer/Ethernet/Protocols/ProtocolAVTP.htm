Protocol Information [▲ back]

The network protocol AVTP (Audio Video Transport Protocol) will facilitate interoperability between stations that stream time-sensitive audio and/or video across LANs providing time synchronization and latency/bandwidth services. AVTP is defined in IEEE 1722.
