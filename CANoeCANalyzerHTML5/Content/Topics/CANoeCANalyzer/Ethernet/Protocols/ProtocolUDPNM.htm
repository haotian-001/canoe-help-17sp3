Protocol Information [▲ back]

UDPNetwork Management (UDP NM) is a protocol from automotive sector for the management of a network, e.g. for the coordination of the transition between normal operation and sleep mode or to determine all active nodes.

Programming [▲ back]

You can access the UDP NM protocol of an Ethernet packet via the selector (CAPL) or the UDPNM property (.NET).

Initialization

During initialization of the UDP NM protocol, the fields SourcePort and DestinationPort in the UDP header are also set to a UDP NM port number that is configured in the Options dialog.
