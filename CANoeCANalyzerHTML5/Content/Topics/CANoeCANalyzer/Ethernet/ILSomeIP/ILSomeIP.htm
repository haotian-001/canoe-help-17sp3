SOME/IP Interaction Layer (SOME/IP IL)

 

When you want to receive and send SOME/IP messages from a CAPL program of a simulated node, you must include the SOME/IP Interaction Layer (SOME/IP IL). Then you can receive and send SOME/IP messages.
