Option AFDX

 

AFDX (ARINC-664) is an Ethernet-based protocol (full duplex, 100 MBit/s). To assure deterministic behavior, there are specific extensions such as limited frame sizes, sequence numbers and requirements related to time behavior.

For redundancy purposes, two Ethernet channels (Line-A and Line-B) are also normally bundled into one AFDX channel to increase fault tolerance.

Application Area: Networking in avionics industry.
