VN8900

 

General

The VN8900 combines the ease of use (plug & play, USB â as e.g. VN1600) with realtime capability. For that purpose in CANoe the realtime-relevant parts of the simulation- and test environment are executed on the VN8900 device (distributed mode/standalone mode). Thereby you achieve improvements with respect to latency times and precision of timers.

The device has four network interfaces that can be used to connect CAN, LIN or FlexRay transceivers (CANpiggy/LINpiggy). Additional network devices (e.g. with FlexRay support) can be connected to the VN8900 directly via the USB port. The device itself is also connected to the computer via USB/IP.

The term VN8900 encompasses all units and variants of the device. The variants of the base unit (VN8910/VN8910A/VN8911/VN8912/VN8912A/VN8914) and of the plugin module (VN8950/VN8970/VN8972) are distinguished from one another by means of the last two places in the VN number. In principle they do not differ in their usage â apart from minor exceptions.

The processor in the device has enough performance capacity to enable the simulation environment (based on Windows) to process even the more demanding models with higher throughput volumes.

Please refer to the device handbook for more information on how to set up, use and configure the VN8900 (e.g. swapping transceivers).
