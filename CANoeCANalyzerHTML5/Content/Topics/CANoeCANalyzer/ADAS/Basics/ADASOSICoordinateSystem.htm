OSI Coordinate System

 

DetectedObjects

The position of a DetectedObject is specified in relation to the Virtual Mounting Position according to OSI3. The virtual mounting position itself is specified in relation to the selected vehicle coordinate system.

Furthermore, it is assumed that the vehicle coordinate system is always centered on the rear axle of the vehicle.

To obtain the position of all detected objects in relation to the vehicle system, the position and orientation of the objects must be corrected by the position and orientation of the virtual mounting position.

For this purpose, the Virtual Mounting Position is read out within the Sensor Data (Top Level message) in the Scene Window, for example.

Note

For the pure display of the sensor cone in the Scene Window, the Virtual Mounting Position is evaluated within the SensorViewConfiguration (Top Level message). For a complete representation, the fields in both messages must be filled correctly.

 

GroundTruth

The position of a GroundTruth object, especially the MovingObject, is specified with respect to the center of the object, unless otherwise specified. The positions are given in relation to the world coordinate system.

For the conversion of a GroundTruth object into the vehicle coordinate system, e.g. in the scene window, the HostVehicle must be specified. In addition, the offset from the vehicle center to the vehicle coordinate system must be specified in this. For this the field bbcenter_to_rear is evaluated within the VehicleAttributes.
