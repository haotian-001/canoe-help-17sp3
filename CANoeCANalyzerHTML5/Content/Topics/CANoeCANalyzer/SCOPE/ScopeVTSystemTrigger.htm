Triggering with VT System

Devices and Triggers Page|Context Menu|Add Trigger Condition|I/O Trigger…

Preconditions

You need a T connector cable which is connected to the Sync channel of VT System. On the double ended side the cable is connected to the scope device on the Ext. and Gen. inputs. See labels on connectors.

Activate VT System Trigger Conditions in CANoe

You have to define a special I/O trigger condition for VT System hardware on the Devices and Triggers (Scope) page of the Scope Window. This trigger condition allows you to trigger physical signals of the VT System. E.g. sensor signals based on PSI5 or SENT protocol; see page I/O Trigger for details.

When a CANoe measurement is running you have to activate the configured trigger condition by pressing the Activate trigger toolbar button . If you want to trigger repetitive times you should switch to Repeat in the toolbar of the Scope Window.
