Bus Bars

 

The module has two bus bars. You can switch line a of each of the 12 normally open contact channels to them.

You cannot switch line a to bus bars a and b at the same time, i.e. you can only switch it to one of the two bus bars at a given time.
This prevents short circuits from occurring when e.g. one bus bar is connected to Vbatt and the other to ground.
