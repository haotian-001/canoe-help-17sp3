VT2820 – General-Purpose Relay Module

 

General

The VT2820 General-Purpose Relay Module has 20 channels, of which 12 are normally open contacts and eight are changeover contacts. With the normally open contact channels, you can also switch line a to two bus bars.

The channels are laid out so that they can carry and switch currents of up to 8 A as well as higher-frequency signals, e.g. 1 Mbaud CAN signals.

The switching state of each channel is indicated by a LED on the front panel.

Switching Different Signal Paths

Functions and Technical Data

With channels 1-12, you can switch line a either to line b or to one of the two bus bars, a or b.

You can switch channels 13-20 between line a and lines b or c.

To switch the relays, you use the corresponding system variables of each channel.

Notice!

The maximum permissible current for both switching and constant currents in closed states is 8 A.
