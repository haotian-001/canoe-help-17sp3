Protocol Overview China

 

A large number of protocols used in the Chinese automotive sector are supported by CANoe. Therefore, the detail view of the Trace Window includes a detailed interpretation of the protocol information of each Car2x packet. In addition, programmatic access to protocol information of Car2x packets from CAPL nodes is possible.
