AID and PSID

 

AID (Application Identifier) and PSID (Provider Service Identifier) are two names for the same ID. The term AID is used in the frame of ETSI GeoNetworking, the PSID is used in the frame of IEEE WAVE/DSRC. The IDs are unambiguous and are managed by the ISO. The complete list of AIDs and PSIDs can be found on the homepage of the ISO (view link address https://standards.iso.org/iso/ts/17419/TS17419%20Assigned%20Numbers/TS17419_ITS-AID_AssignedNumbers.pdf).

The IDs are mainly used for three purposes:

  • The IDs show the content of higher protocol layers in a transport protocol
  • By means of a PKI and with the help of certificates and the IDs the permissions of an ITS station is determined.
  • A provider announces its own provided services via the IDs.
