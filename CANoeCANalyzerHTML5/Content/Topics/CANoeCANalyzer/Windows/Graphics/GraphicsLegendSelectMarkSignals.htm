Graphics Window: Selecting and Marking Symbols

 

For a better understanding the terms marking and selecting shall be defined in the following:

Selection

One symbol at most is selected in the legend at any given time. The selected symbol is highlighted graphically in the legend. In the Alternating Y-axes view (Y-Axis Views) it is the axis of the selected symbol that is shown in the diagram.

In the figure CarSpeed is the selected symbol.

Marking

Marking allows you to choose one or more symbols in order to enlarge, reduce or fit them. Multiple marking is intended for working with a small subset of the symbols for a brief period of time.

Mark the symbols in the legend or in the diagram by selecting the symbols successively while holding down the shift or control key.

In the figure CarSpeed and Gear are the marked symbols. Additionally Gear is the selected symbol.

Note

  • A marked symbol may also be selected.
  • The measuring functions only affect the marked symbols.
  • The measurement functions act exclusively on marked symbols.
  • In single graph mode all commands (e.g. measure, zoom, scroll,...) refer to the marked symbols.
  • In all graphs mode all commands refer to all symbols.
  • You have the option of selecting/marking one symbol in the diagram by directly selecting the curve.
