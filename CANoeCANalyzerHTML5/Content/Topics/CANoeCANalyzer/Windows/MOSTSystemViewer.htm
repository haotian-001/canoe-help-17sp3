MOST System Viewer

Ribbon|Analysis Tab|System Viewer

The MOST System Viewer provides an overview of all connected MOST devices. To obtain this information, the MOST System Viewers the ring scan of the MOST NetworkMaster and other events on the MOST ring.

In addition, the MOST System Viewer offers the option to retrieve the desired information actively from individual control units or all control units. To exercise this option, select the Scan command in the menu bar for the entire ring or in the context menu for an individual node.

For better recognition of individual control units, you can assign a name to the node. In the case of changing ring configurations, the control unit at the respective position can be identified automatically based on the logged-on function blocks.

Position and configuration of the MOST interface(s)

In addition to the information listed above, a Vector interface is displayed with a red border.

Statistics

The Statistics View displays the utilization of the MOST ring.

Detailed information on the individual control units is displayed underneath. You can switch to the individual devices by selecting a device in the ring display or in the selection list of the Statistics View.

Option to navigate to other windows (MOST FBlock Monitor, Stress, Register Editor)

You can switch to other analysis or simulation windows via the context menu for the individual devices:

MOST Stress...
(The Stress Window is brought to the foreground, and the destination address for the busload generation is set to the selected device.)

MOST FBlock Monitor...
(The MOST FBlock Monitor is brought to the foreground, and the display is switched to the properties of the selected device.)

MOST Register Editor...
(If a control unit contains an OS8104, registers can be viewed or written using the Register Editor.)
